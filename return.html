Returns Policy

Effective Date: 1 OCTOBER 2024

We take pride in the quality of our handcrafted items, but if you’re not satisfied, we’re here to help.

1. Return Eligibility
Returns are accepted within 30 days of receiving your order. Items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items
We cannot accept returns for the following:

  • Customized or personalized orders.
  • Items marked as final sale.
